Features

A complete mockup workflow from design upload to final export — all in one tool.

AI Background Removal
Powered by rembg (U-2-Net AI). Remove backgrounds from any image in 1-2 seconds, completely offline. No subscription to remove.bg needed.

Bulk Generation
Apply all your designs to all your templates in one click. Generate 100 mockups in the time it takes to make one manually.

Custom Masking
Draw precise clipping masks so your design wraps perfectly around edges, pockets, seams — no overflow outside the product boundary.

How it works

From design to mockup in 4 simple steps

No learning curve. Open the studio, follow the flow, get professional results.

1
Upload Template
Add any product photo as your background template — t-shirt, mug, frame, pillow, anything.
2
Set Warp Points
Drag the 4 corner handles to define the print area with perfect perspective. Draw a mask if needed.
3
Upload Designs
Drop your design files (logos, graphics, illustrations) — upload many at once.
4
Generate & Download
Hit Generate. Download your production-ready mockups instantly from the Gallery.
